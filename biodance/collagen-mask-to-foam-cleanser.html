EN: 2-IN-1 CLEANSER & PORE CARE. Combining the power of a foam cleanser and a pore-refining mask, this innovative 2-in-1 formula effortlessly removes impurities while improving skin texture. DIRECTIONS FOR USE: AS A DEEP CLEANSING MASK: 1. Apply to dry skin, avoiding the eyes and lips. 2. Leave on for 1–3 minutes, then massage with wet hands. 3. Rinse with lukewarm water. AS A DAILY FOAM CLEANSER: 1. Lather a coin-sized amount with water. 2. Massage onto damp skin for 60 seconds until a creamy foam forms. 3. Rinse with lukewarm water.
