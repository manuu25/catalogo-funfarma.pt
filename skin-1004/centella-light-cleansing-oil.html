Centella Light Cleansing Oil

PT: Centella e 6 óleos de origem vegetal dissolvem suavemente a maquiagem, o protetor solar e o excesso de sebo da pele, deixando um acabamento refrescante e sem oleosidade. MODO DE USAR: Aplique alguns pumps do óleo de limpeza no rosto seco, com as mãos secas. Massageie suavemente em movimentos circulares para dissolver a maquiagem e as impurezas. Adicione um pouco de água e continue massageando para criar uma emulsão leve e leitosa. Enxague completamente com água morna.


EN: Centella and 6 plant-derived oils gently dissolve makeup, sunscreen and excess sebum on the skin, leaving a refreshing, oil-free finish. DIRECTIONS FOR USE: Apply a few pumps of cleansing oil onto dry face with dry hands. Gently massage in circular motions to dissolve makeup and debris. Add a little water and continue massaging to create a light and milky emulsion. Rinse off completely with lukewarm water.


INGREDIENTES: Ethylhexyl Stearate, Cetyl Ethylhexanoate, Sorbeth-30 Tetraoleate, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Citrus Aurantium Bergamia (Bergamot) Fruit Oil, Centella Asiatica Extract, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Olea Europaea (Olive) Fruit Oil, Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil, Ethylhexylglycerin, Pelargonium Graveolens Flower Oil, Rosa Damascena Flower Oil, Limonene, Linalool
