Calming Serum: Green tea + Panthenol

PT: Um sérum refrescante e calmante para aliviar instantaneamente a pele estressada ou sensibilizada. Formulado com alta concentração de extrato de artemísia (mugwort) e chá verde para reduzir a aparência de vermelhidão. MODO DE USAR: Coloque algumas gotas nas mãos limpas e dê leves batidinhas na pele até total absorção. Use de manhã e à noite, evitando encostar o conta-gotas diretamente na pele para manter a fórmula íntegra.


EN: A cooling, calming serum to instantly soothe stressed or reactive skin. Made with a high concentration of mugwort extract and green tea to reduce the look of redness. DIRECTONS FOR USE: Dispense a few drops onto clean hands and gently pat into the skin until absorbed. Use morning and night, and avoid touching the dropper directly to keep the formula clean.


INGREDIENTES: Camellia Sinensis Leaf Water, Water, Panthenol, Glycerin, Butylene Glycol, 1,2-Hexanediol, Methylpropanediol, Acrylates/C10-30 Alkyl Acrylate Crosspolymer, Tromethamine, Betaine, Ethylhexylglycerin, Allantoin, Dipotassium Glycyrrhizate, Artemisia Capillaris Extract, Glyceryl Acrylate/Acrylic Acid Copolymer, Melia Azadirachta Leaf Extract, Polyquaternium-51, Malt Extract, Melia Azadirachta Flower Extract, Coptis Japonica Root Extract, Sodium Hyaluronate
