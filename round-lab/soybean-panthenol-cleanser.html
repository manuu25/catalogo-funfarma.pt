Soybean Panthenol Cleanser

PT: O Soybean Panthenol Low pH Cleanser é um gel de limpeza suave que purifica a pele sem remover a hidratação essencial. Formulado com extrato de soja fermentada cultivada em Jeongseon, pantenol (Vitamina B5) e um pH amigo da pele de 5,5, remove eficazmente sujidade, suor e maquilhagem ligeira, enquanto promove uma barreira cutânea saudável. A espuma macia e envolvente deixa a pele fresca, hidratada e suavizada ideal para a limpeza diária, de manhã ou à noite. MODO DE UTILIZAÇÃO: Aplique uma quantidade adequada nas mãos molhadas. Esfregue até criar uma espuma rica e massaje suavemente no rosto húmido. Enxague bem com água morna.


EN: The Soybean Panthenol Low pH Cleanser is a gentle foaming cleanser that purifies skin without stripping away essential moisture. Formulated with Jeongseon-grown fermented soybean extract, panthenol (Vitamin B5), and a skin-friendly pH of 5.5, it effectively removes dirt, sweat, and light makeup while supporting a healthy skin barrier. The soft, cushiony foam leaves skin feeling refreshed, hydrated, and soothed-perfect for daily cleansing, morning or night. DIRECTIONS FOR USE: Dispense an appropriate amount into wet hands. Work into a rich foam and gently massage onto damp face. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.


INGREDIENTES: Water(Aqua), Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate, Glycerin, Lauryl Betaine, Glyceryl Stearate, Stearyl Alcohol, Sodium Methyl Cocoyl Taurate, Sodium Polyacrylate, Citric Acid, Glycine Max (Soybean) Seed Extract, Butylene Glycol, Sodium Phytate, 1,2- Hexanediol, Dipropylene Glycol, Panthenol, Hydrogenated Lecithin, Cetearyl Olivate, Sorbitan Olivate, Ethylhexylglycerin, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Polyglyceryl-10 Stearate, Ceramide NP, Cholesterol, Stearic Acid.
